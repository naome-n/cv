RESEARCH PROJECTS

  • Spring 2019 Research Proposal on Modeled Pharmacokinetics of a Contrast Agent in Patients with Anemia
    • Proposes to expand the SDE model made by Susanne Ditlevsen and Adeline Samson to include patients who suffer from anemia in which they have a significantly lower hematocrit level.
    • Explore how the contrast agent concentration would change along time if the arteries are blocked or damaged
    • Analyze how the pharmacokinetic model would change with different assumptions, using the stochastic approach to account for random fluctuations
  • Spring 2018 Research Project How Cosmetics with Microbeads Affect Water Quality
    • Hypothesis: When cosmetic microbeads are drained into the water, it lowers the water quality index of the water.
    • Analyzed the total phosphates, dissolved oxygen (DO), pH, and colony forming units (CFU) of all our control and experimental groups and reported data using descriptive and inferential statistics.
